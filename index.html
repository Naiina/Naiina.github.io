University of Edinburgh
Sept 2023 – ongoing · Edinburgh
PhD · NLP and Psycholinguistics: Cognitive constraints shaping syntactic structures cross-linguistically
Supervisors: Edoardo Ponti, Sharon Goldwater

Investigating fine-grained interactions between semantics, vision and syntax in a quantitative way, using AI to create, modify, and annotate large cross-linguistic corpora.

  • Demonstrated that animacy consistently influences grammar across all languages covered by a fine-tuned animacy classifier (BabelNet + BERT/Aya), generalising previous findings beyond specific constructions and languages.
  • Performed a causal analysis of the impact of visual saliency on syntax in picture descriptions, using Vision-Language Models (VLMs) to intervene on saliency features of entities in images.
  • Currently investigating grammatical variations of events in English and Chinese video captions.
